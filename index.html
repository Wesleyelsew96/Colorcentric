How to play:

  • Use your arrow keys to move all the tiles one space each
  • Move matching tiles to the middle to keep the board clear
  • When the middle is matched, a new center color appears
  • New tiles land in the corners - keep your corners clear!
  • Clear two tiles simultaneously to clear all tiles of that color
  • The "Next tile" color also shows the next center tile color
