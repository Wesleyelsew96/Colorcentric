How to play: Use your arrow keys to move all the tiles one space. Move the correct tiles to the middle to keep the board clear. When a tile is moved to the middle, a color appears in the center. The color at the top shows the next tile (and center color) to appear. New tiles land in the corners - make sure to always keep a corner clear. See if you can get the rainbow tile before the board fills up!
